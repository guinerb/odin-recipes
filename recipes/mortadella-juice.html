Mortadella Juice

Description:

Mortadella Juice

Ingredients:

  • 1 Liter of Milk
  • 500 grams of Mortadella

Steps:

  1. Slice the mortadella in small pieces
  2. Turn on your juicer, add the milk then the mortadella pieces one at a time. Turn off the machine and pour the juice into a glass and serve.
